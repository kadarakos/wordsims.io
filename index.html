WordSims.io word similarity benchmarking

Simple web app to benchmark vectorial word representations on the most common word similarity/relatedness benchmarks. Upload your vectors in space separated format "word 0.0 0.35 1.0", choose the similarity metric and click on the blue button. The app calculates the Spearmanâ€™s rho between word-vector similarities and human similarity judgments. The confidence intervals are calculated using bootstrap provided by the scikit-bootstrap module.
